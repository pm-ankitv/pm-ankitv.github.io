Situation:

Mi Pay began as a strategic white space within Xiaomi's ecosystem—a vision to build a payments platform that could seamlessly integrate with Xiaomi's broader commerce and service ambitions. At inception, there was no team, infrastructure, or regulatory clearance.

Task:

My mandate was to conceptualize, build, and scale a digital payments business from the ground up—developing a full-stack UPI platform, establishing compliance with Indian regulators, and transforming Mi Pay into a revenue-generating pillar of Xiaomi's ecosystem.

Action:

  • Built the team from scratch—from being the first hire to leading a 28-member cross-functional unit, including mentoring 4 Product Managers and 6 Management Trainees. Established a KPI-driven performance model to align team output with strategic business goals.
  • Architected a scalable platform aligned with a holistic ENABLE, ACQUIRE, ENGAGE, EDUCATE, ENCASH strategy:
    • • Enable: Integrated UPI and payment gateways, and empowered Customer Success teams with faster resolution tools—enhancing trust, NPS, and user satisfaction.
    • • Acquire: Drove user acquisition through OS-level deep integration and contextual surface placements across Xiaomi devices.
    • • Engage & Educate: Leveraged in-app communities, A/B testing, and feedback loops to iterate features and increase product stickiness; used this as a channel to onboard and educate users.
    • • Encash: Monetized the 60M+ user base with non-intrusive ad-tech and contextual product placements—achieving net positive P&L.
  • Led stakeholder collaboration across regulators (NPCI, RBI), banking partners, and internal teams (Legal, Compliance, Marketing, Ecosystem), ensuring agility without compromising compliance.
  • Successfully managed 28 regulatory and security audits in five years, covering VA-PT, PCI-DSS, ISO 27001, and Data Localization, maintaining Xiaomi's license to operate in a highly regulated market.

Result:

  • Scaled Mi Pay to 60 million registered users, 30 million MAUs, and 3 million DAUs, making it one of India's leading embedded payments platforms.
  • Delivered a sustainable and compliant payments business that became a strategic revenue contributor, tightly integrated with Xiaomi's broader product and commerce ecosystem.
